
## 2026-04-15 - Fix Hardcoded API Key Leakage\n**Vulnerability:** The API client in `scripts/api_client.py` was printing the first 8 characters of the `RDW_APP_TOKEN` to the console output.\n**Learning:** Even printing prefixes of secrets for debugging purposes can leak sensitive information to CI/CD logs. Security scanners or attackers could potentially piece together or exploit leaked prefixes if the tokens are short or follow a predictable pattern.\n**Prevention:** Always fully obfuscate secrets in log messages (e.g., `***`) or avoid logging them entirely, even partially.
